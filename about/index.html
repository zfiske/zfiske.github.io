About Me

As a runner, I am captivated by historic races and routes. While I have never been particularly motivated by speed, challenges of extreme endurance have always resonated with me for the stories they tell and the thrill of the unknown. I am always seeking new adventures and plotting unique landscapes to explore under the power of my own two feet... there are many paths yet untrodden, and I can't wait to share what comes next.

I post live updates with pictures and stories from my adventures on Instagram, and during projects this is usually the fastest way to reach me. You can also (occasionally) find me on Strava, whenever I dig my old watch out of the closet to record a race or project run.
