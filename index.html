Howdy!

As a runner, I am captivated by historic races and routes. While I have never been particularly motivated by speed, challenges of extreme endurance have always resonated with me for the stories they tell and the thrill of the unknown. I am always seeking new adventures and plotting unique landscapes to explore under the power of my own two feet.

This site is a place for me to share my projects - you can check out some of my short stories, racing adventures, and follow current and future journeys live. Feel free to reach out about any of my projects, I'd love to hear from you and I can't wait to share what comes next.
